genuine culture and exciting people

Explore a variety of amazing places

Did you know Houston is one of America's top foodie cities? And after grabbing a nice lunch, you can head over to catch a play in the theatre district, relive history in the museum district, or learn about the newest discoveries in space at NASA!

Meet a diverse group of people

Houston is a melting pot of culture with over 90 languages spoken. That means our music, arts, and street scene is really cool. And nested in the middle of it all is Rice University, home of Edgar Odell Lovett College, the best residential college of them all.

sports domination

The Astros claim the 2017 World Series Championship

In one of the most hard fought finals of all time, the Houston Astros defeat the Los Angeles Dodgers in seven games to win their first World Series title in franchise history. They were led by outfielder and unanimous MVP George Springer, who had a two-run home run in the deciding game.

The Rockets fight for revenge in the Western Conference

Clippers point guard Chris Paul joined the Rockets and formed a formidable duo with superstar James Harden to lead the Houston Rockets to the NBA western conference finals, where they lost in a 7 game series to the Golden State Warriors. This year, they fight for a rematch to take down the reigning champs.
