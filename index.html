Explore a variety of amazing places

Did you know Houston is one of America's top foodie cities? From the Vietnamese Banh Mi to the famous Tex-Mex, Houston offers a variety of authentic and delicious cuisine. And after grabbing a nice lunch, you can head over to catch a play in the theatre district, relive history in the museum district, or learn about the newest discoveries in space at NASA!

Meet a diverse group of people

Houston is a melting pot of culture with over 90 languages spoken. That means our music, arts, and street scene is really cool. And nested in the middle of it all is Rice University, home of Edgar Odell Lovett College. It's your chance to meet inspirational figures like Scott Rixner and Luay Nakhleh, who will teach you about algorithms, object-oriented programming, and the meaning of life.
